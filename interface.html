The Global Crisis

The Scale of Microplastic Contamination

Microplastics are ubiquitous in our oceans and water systems. Traditional manual detection methods via microscopy are labor-intensive, prone to human error, and cannot scale to meet the crisis.

public 14 Million Tons

Estimated amount of microplastics residing on the ocean floor, contaminating deep-sea ecosystems previously thought pristine.

health_and_safety Bioaccumulation

manage_search Detection Gap

Current spectroscopic methods are slow. Manual counting has a 30% error rate. We need automated, high-throughput solutions.

Ensemble Learning Architecture

Our "Dual-Stream" approach combines the efficiency of EfficientNet-B0 with the depth of ResNet-50, fused through a custom weighted averaging layer.
